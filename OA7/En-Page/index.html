SURVEYING AND MAPPING

We offer a service focused on graphic and technical representation through topographic, geodesic, and bathymetric surveys of a surface, with all the forms and details to be able to plan and develop any project. Likewise, we generate the plans and calculations required for our clients, such as volumes, level curves, longitudinal profiles, cross sections, and detailed plans, among others.

TECHNOLOGICAL SOLUTIONS

We use all available knowledge and technology to develop technical and regulatory solutions that best meet the needs of our customers or the industry. In addition, we use drone technology capable of capturing immediate graphic information on construction elements, the main advantage of which is the ability to do so in high places, around moving elements, in inaccessible spaces, and outdoors. They are useful in the construction of civil structures and building structures, both for auditing and control tasks during the construction phase as well as for inspection and maintenance of the works carried out.
