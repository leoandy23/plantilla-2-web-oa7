About us?

OA7 Ingenierías is a company dedicated to providing engineering services specializing in the use of geospatial technologies. We offer geopositioning solutions, geospatial technology, and GNSS systems for engineering, construction, and cadastre. Our priority is to innovate to offer safety, productivity, and efficiency to our customers. We are highly qualified professionals, and we comply with national and municipal regulations.

Mission

Our mission is to use geospatial technologies to benefit the community.
